The twists in the story

There are twists in the story

Look beneath the expected cutbacks. Three anomalies surface — not retreat, but quiet rewriting of the rules on trust, joy and expertise.

  • 01

    Trust shifted,
    it didn't collapse

    Banks stopped being the enemy — even as government slid to the floor. Confidence in the NHS stands at 6.42 / 10, yet 53% say it has declined. High regard and falling confidence, side by side.

  • 02

    Joy was ring-fenced,
    not abandoned

    Fashion and the pub flex. The holiday is defended like a bill that must be paid — 40% protect it at all costs. The lipstick effect is real.

  • 03

    Expertise is now a prompt away

    What professionals once gatekept — money, health, the law — is on tap. 58% have used AI instead of a human professional for at least one task.
