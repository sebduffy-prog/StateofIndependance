There are twists in the story

Look beneath the expected cutbacks and three anomalies surface. This is not a nation retreating into a bunker — it is quietly rewriting the rules.

  • 01

    Trust shifted, it didn't collapse

    Banks stopped being the enemy—even as faith in government slid to the floor.

  • 02

    Joy was ring-fenced, not abandoned

    Fashion and the pub flex. The holiday is defended like a bill that must be paid.

  • 03

    Expertise is now a prompt away

    What professionals once gatekept—money, health, the law—is on tap.
