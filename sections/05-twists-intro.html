Chapter four

There are twists in the story

Beneath the expected cutbacks, three anomalies emerge. This is not a population retreating into a bunker. It is rewriting the rules of survival, institutional trust and technology.

  • 01

    Trust shifted,
    not collapsed

    Banks stopped being the enemy, even as government slid to the floor. The NHS scores 6.42 / 10, yet 53% say its performance has declined. High regard and falling confidence, side by side.

  • 02

    Joy was ring-fenced,
    not abandoned

    Fashion and the pub flex. The holiday is defended like a bill that must be paid: 40% protect it at all costs. The lipstick effect is real.

  • 03

    Expertise is now a
    prompt away

    What professionals once gatekept, money, health, the law, is now on tap. 58% have used AI instead of a human expert for at least one task.
