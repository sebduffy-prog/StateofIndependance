The rest of the floor

Three more numbers everyone already knows

Deal-hunting, low-grade dread, the quiet downgrade at the till. Read each one in turn — the squeeze is the table the country eats at, not the meal.

55%

have hunted out deals and shopped around in the last three months.

The thrift reflex is now muscle memory — shopping around is the first move, not the last resort.

60%

feel anxious about the coming months.

Dread sits just below carefulness — the weather the whole survey is taken in.

54%

have traded down to cheaper groceries this year.

More than half quietly swapped the brand for the own-label — the basket shrank without a word.
